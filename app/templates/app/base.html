Exclusive Raid Gym Tracker - Leeds

A tool for tracking which gyms you've visited in order to maximise you chance at getting an exclusive raid in the city center of Leeds.

How it works

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Add the date of the last raid you did for the Gyms in the town centre
  3. Use the list to keep on top of the gyms so you don't miss out on an exclusive raid

Product Roadmap

  • See when a raid is active on a gym you need to do a raid on
  • See how many others need to do a raid on a particular gym - so you can organise a raid party
